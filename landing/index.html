What we hold

Craftsmanship

Every system we build carries the weight of its decisions visibly. No hidden trade-offs. No shortcuts dressed up as features. The work earns its complexity or sheds it.

Transformation

A forge changes the nature of what enters it. We don't optimize what's broken — we re-examine what's assumed. The best infrastructure starts from honest foundations.

Stewardship

Building for the people who use the thing, not the people who sell it. User sovereignty isn't a marketing line. It's an architectural constraint that shapes every decision.

Coherence

Clarity that runs all the way through — from the documentation to the design to the byte on disk. If it can't be explained plainly, it hasn't been understood well enough to build.

CambiOS

In development

A ground-up operating system — microkernel in Rust, capability-based security, cryptographic identity, zero-trust IPC. No telemetry. No backdoors. Boots on x86_64, AArch64, and RISC-V (in progress). 447 tests passing. Built to prove that the fundamental architecture of computing can be replaced, not just patched. →
